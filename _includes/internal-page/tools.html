Rights of domestic workers

SERI has developed a user-friendly resource guide to create awareness of the rights of domestic workers and the obligations of employers in terms of the domestic employment relationship. It explains what the law says about domestic workers and gives practical advice on how domestic workers can engage with their employers.

Income comparison

If you think about your household, how rich or poor do you think you are compared to other South Africans? SALDRU has developed an income comparison tool to help you see how income is distributed from the poorest to the richest in South Africa. Find out where you are in South Africa's income distribution and how you compare to the rest of the population.
